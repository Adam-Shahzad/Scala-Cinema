Getting There

QA cinemas is situated at the QA consulting training academy in Salford Quays. It is easily accessed via public transport which runs frequently. It is roughly 3 miles from Manchester city centre and only a 10 minute walk to MediaCityUk which hosts a range of shops and eateries.

Find us at:

Anchorage 1, Anchorage Quay, Salford Quays, Salford, Manchester M50 3YJ

By Car: There is parking opposite the building for £9 a day. Or parking at the lowry outlet multi story car park. There is also free parking on roads around the area if you can find them.

By Bus: The 33, 147 and 53 are all frequent buses travelling from town and take about 30 minutes. From salford the 50 is also a regular bus that drops off nearby.

By Train: If coming by train it would be best to travel to Picadilly station and then get the tram to the anchorage stop, see below.

By Tram: Anchorage station is directly across the road. Accessible from Piccadilly on the E line towards Eccles.

Cycling: There is a cycle hub in MediaCityUK to safely contain your bike. Mobikes are also available around the city for only £50p a ride.
